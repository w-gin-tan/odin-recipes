Scallion Pancakes

Description

A scallion pancake is the Chinese version of the classic American breakfast. This pancake is crisp on the outside, fluffy on the inside, and wonderfully savory all over. If youâ€™re looking for a new twist on your regular pancakes, this is the recipe for you.

Ingredients

    For the dough:

  • 300g (10.5 oz) of all-purpose flour use
  • 210ml (0.9 cup/7oz) of warm water
  • 1/2 tsp of salt

    • For the roux:

  • 2 tbsp of all-purpose flour
  • 60ml (1/4 cup) of vegetable oil

    • For the sauce:

  • 4 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp black vinegar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 3 tbsp chopped spring onions
  • 1 tsp chili oil (Optional)

    • Others:

  • Some chopped green onion (about 3-4 stalks)
  • Salt to sprinkle on the dough

Steps

  1. Combine dough ingredients in a mixing bowl. Mix well with a spatula.
  2. Continue mixing (or with an electric mixer with the dough hook attached) until it is smooth and stay free from the bowl. Cover and rest for 30 minutes.
  3. Mix roux ingredients in a small saucepan. Heat until the oil starts boiling to form the roux. Set aside to cool.
  4. Divide the dough into four. Roll each portion thinly into a long rectangle.
  5. Apply the roux on the surface but leave about one cm free from the edges.
  6. Sprinkle the salt and spring onion from the other ingredients on the surface.
  7. Roll up the dough like making a Swiss roll. Roll and stretch the dough to form a long rope.
  8. Coil the rope to form a round dough.
  9. Roll out to about 1/4 inch thick circle. Pan-fried over medium heat until it has charred lines and speckled spots.
  10. Flip over and cover the pan for one minute. Remove the cover and continue pan-frying until both sides are golden brown. Remove from heat.
  11. Combine all sauce ingredients in a sauce bowl.
